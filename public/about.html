Built for developers who grind after dark

AfterHours is a focused interview prep tracker — 300 curated DSA problems across 20 patterns, designed for people who prep when the day job is done.

Why I built this

I created AfterHours because most prep tools feel either too scattered or too generic. I wanted one place to work through a structured pattern list, mark progress, and actually come back the next night without losing momentum.

This isn't about grinding LeetCode blindly — it's about building pattern recognition, tracking what you've solved, and revisiting problems when you need a refresh.

What makes it different

  • 20 patterns, 300 problems — LeetCode classics plus GFG, InterviewBit & more
  • Progress that persists — streaks, breakdowns, and pattern-level stats
  • Smart nudges — insights on what to solve next based on your gaps
  • Motivation on every win — celebrate done and redo moments
  • After-hours vibe — built for late-night sessions, not corporate dashboards

How to use it

  1. Create an account and pick a pattern from the sidebar
  2. Solve on LeetCode, then mark problems done as you finish
  3. Check your Progress page for streaks and weak spots
  4. Uncheck problems when you want to redo them — we'll cheer you on

Ready to start?

Your progress only counts when you're signed in. One problem tonight is enough to begin.
