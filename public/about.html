Why I built this

I was tired of opening LeetCode, solving something, closing the tab, and forgetting where I left off a week later. Spreadsheet trackers felt like homework. Big prep platforms felt built for full-time students — not for someone showing up after standup and code reviews.

I didn't need another 500-problem list. I needed one clear path, real progress I could see, and a reason to show up tomorrow night.

So I built AfterHours: a focused tracker around patterns, not random problem hopping. Solve on LeetCode, come back here, check it off, and watch your streak and badges grow. That's it. No noise.
