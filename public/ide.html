Why this IDE?

Think in your own test cases first.

On LeetCode, the editor and test cases are already set up for you. You mostly slot in an answer and hit submit.

This IDE is for the step before that — sketch your approach, type your own inputs in stdin, run, tweak, and break things on purpose until the logic actually holds.

That back-and-forth builds stronger cognitive thinking and execution. You are not guessing against hidden cases; you are proving the idea on cases you chose.

  1. Pick a problem from Solve.
  2. Draft code here with inputs you care about.
  3. Run, adjust, rerun until it feels solid.
  4. Then open LeetCode for the final attempt.
